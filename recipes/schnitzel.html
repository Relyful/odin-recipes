Schnitzel

Description

Pork Schnitzels are breaded and fried to perfection for an easy and delicious meal the whole family will love. Total cozy comfort food!

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Salt & pepper
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Whisker eggs in a container
  2. Put flour in a container and bread crumbs into another container
  3. Put the pork chop into the egg and then into the flour and then into the bread crumbs
  4. Cook in a pan until light brown
  5. Enjoy!
